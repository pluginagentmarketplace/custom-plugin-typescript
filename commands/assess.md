# Assess

Evaluate your current skills and knowledge level across the Developer Roadmap tracks.

## Skill Assessment Framework

This assessment helps you identify your current level in each of the 7 specialization tracks. Be honest in your self-assessment for accurate results.

## Assessment Levels

### Level 1: Beginner
- Just starting out in this area
- Limited practical experience
- Knowledge mainly from courses
- Comfortable with 20% of the concepts
- Would need significant support for production work

### Level 2: Foundational
- Basic understanding of core concepts
- Some hands-on experience
- Can work on simple projects with guidance
- Comfortable with 40% of the concepts
- Could contribute to production with supervision

### Level 3: Intermediate
- Solid understanding of most concepts
- Moderate hands-on experience
- Can work on projects independently
- Comfortable with 60% of the concepts
- Ready for mid-level roles

### Level 4: Advanced
- Deep understanding of concepts
- Extensive hands-on experience
- Can make architectural decisions
- Comfortable with 80% of the concepts
- Ready for senior/lead roles

### Level 5: Expert
- Mastery of the domain
- Years of production experience
- Teaches others and contributes to best practices
- Comfortable with 95%+ of the concepts
- Ready for principal/architect roles
